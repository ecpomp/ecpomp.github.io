Hello, Welcome to DoE's ECP OpenMP Project Page!

SOLLVE is the OpenMP Sub-project in United States Department of Energy's Exascale Computing Project. SOLLVE holds hackathons each year to help application programmers use OpenMP effectively in their application programs. Read more here.
